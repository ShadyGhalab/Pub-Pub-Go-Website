Privacy Policy

Pup Pup Go is built for quick, family-friendly play. This policy explains the basic information that may be handled when you use the app.

Information We Collect

Pup Pup Go does not ask you to create an account inside the app. The app may use Apple services, including Game Center and App Store purchases, which are handled by Apple under Apple's own privacy terms.

Game Center

If you use Game Center features, Apple may process leaderboard, achievement, and player information. You can manage Game Center from your device settings.

Device and App Data

The app may store gameplay progress, settings, and preferences on your device so your levels, stars, sound settings, and similar gameplay state can be remembered.

Children's Privacy

Pup Pup Go is designed to be family-friendly. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children through our own account system.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact support@pubbyparade.com.

Updates

This policy may be updated as the app changes. The latest version will remain available on this page.
